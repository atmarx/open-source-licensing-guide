## Court Cases

### Oracle v. Google Litigation Timeline
<!-- slug: oracle-v-google-litigation-timeline -->
- **2010:** Oracle files suit against Google
- **2012:** District court rules APIs not copyrightable (Judge Alsup)
- **2014:** Federal Circuit reverses, rules APIs copyrightable
- **2016:** Jury finds Google's use is fair use
- **2018:** Federal Circuit reverses fair use finding
- **2021:** Supreme Court rules for Google on fair use grounds (6-2)

Source: Court records and reporting from Ars Technica, The Verge, and legal commentary.
